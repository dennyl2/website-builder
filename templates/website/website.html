About Me

Hello, I'm Denny Lu, a passionate photographer based in Irvine, CA. With roots in the charming town of Temecula, I have grown and found my professional calling in creative photography. My journey began academically at the University of California, Irvine, where I cultivated my artistic skills alongside technical studies. Beyond academics, I honed crucial soft skills while working at McDonald's for five years, where I thrived in fast-paced environments and learned the value of sequencing tasks efficiently. Accolades include a Gold Presidential Service Award and a Congressional Service Award, accomplishments that reflect my dedication, perseverance, and leadership capabilities. Beyond the lens, I am a storyteller at heart, binding the fine threads of composition, lighting, and narrative to weave captivating tales through my images. As I continue to explore and grow in this magnificent craft, my goal is to not only capture moments but create them, infusing each shot with a dynamic and modern elegance.

My Work

My portfolio is a tapestry of diverse photographic styles, encompassing everything from sweeping landscapes to intimate portraits. Each project is approached with a unique perspective, as I strive to push boundaries and explore new possibilities. Significant projects include capturing intricate details of bustling cityscapes and serene rural settings with equal finesse. My work is deeply influenced by my surroundings and experiences, always seeking to portray the beauty and emotion within every frame. With each click of the shutter, my aim is to create an emotional connection, enabling the viewer to experience the subject as intimately as I do. Collaborating with local businesses and private clients in various settings has enriched my journey, providing invaluable insights into professional-grade work. With an eye for detail and a passion for innovation, I constantly seek the perfect balance between artistic expression and client satisfaction.

My Education

My educational journey laid a foundational bedrock for my future endeavors in photography. I graduated from the University of California, Irvine, where I expanded my horizons academically and artistically. UCI provided me with an ambiance replete with creativity and critical thinking, allowing me to immerse myself in various photography projects while understanding their broader context. Prior to this, I graduated with a splendid 4.33 GPA from Great Oak High School, in Temecula. This institution polished my leadership skills, which were recognized through prestigious awards, including two vital leadership awards—Gold Presidential Service Award and Congressional Service Award. These not only acknowledge my academic excellence but also my dedication to community service and leadership. Through education, I learned to appreciate structure and spontaneity in equal measure, which are integral elements that I incorporate into my photographic compositions today.
